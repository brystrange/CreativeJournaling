The Lonely Urban Traveller
A gloomy atmosphere inside one of the buses of the only bus company that operates in the province of Quezon and Batangas. The route starts from the city of Lucena to the city of Batangas, and vice-versa. Various towns will be passed by within its route to destination including the town of Candelaria, Sariaya, and San Juan Batangas to name a few.
