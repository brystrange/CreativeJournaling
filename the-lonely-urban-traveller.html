The Lonely Urban Traveller
A gloomy atmosphere inside one of the buses of the oldest bus company that operates in the province of Quezon and Batangas.
By Bryan Mayor | Monday, May 24, 2021
SUPREME TRANSLINER INC. — the oldest bus company that operates in the province of Quezon and the city of Lucena all the way to the city of Batangas, and vice-versa. Various towns will be passed by within its route to destination including the towns of Candelaria, Sariaya, and San Juan Batangas to name a few. Very little information is known about the company, including their early days and year of establishment.

The bus company has a reputation of having a turtle-like service due to each of the bus' limited capabilities in terms of speed which is known to be at least a decade old each. Despite having a slow service — which affects most day-shift employees — the company never ceases to amaze its passengers with the beauty of the rural community on each trip, especially, the vast land of rice paddies beside the stretch of road of barangay Sta. Catalina in Candelaria and its neighboring barangays where rice is the main source of income for farmers.
Rice paddies beside the national highway of barangay Sta. Catalina in the town of Candelaria, province of Quezon.
The relatively hot yet cold breeze of the wind coming from the open window of the bus and the relaxing scenery of the landscape is one of the few things that you likely want to experience when you roam this part of the province, or happen to pass by coming from the city.
