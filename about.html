"Staring into the nothingness" (2019)

Bryan is currently unemployed and is residing in his family residence in the province of Quezon, Philippines. He loves to read books and make websites. It was when he finished Lemony Snicket's famous book series that Bryan became hooked with writing, and F. Sionil Jose's The Pretenders and F.H. Batacan's Smaller and Smaller Circles that he became attached with Philippine Literature and decided to write more. His short story, "Unexpected Calamities" is currently far from being complete. Though, he admitted that he will put it here, on this website, when it is done. Probably a few years from today.

Whenever he's free, or in an emotional state, he writes poems under the pseudonym, Mr. Poet, which can be seen on Facebook and Hello Poetry.
